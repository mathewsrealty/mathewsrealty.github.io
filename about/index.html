Achievements

Susan Mathews is one of the most accomplished and respected real estate agents in the DFW area. Susan has been recognized in 2018 and 2019 by D Magazine as one of the Top Producers and was the highest producing agent in Westlake in 2019. With over 15 years experience in the business, Susan has had the pleasure to work with top executives, professional athletes, leading attorneys and more. Susan is an Executive Vice President at Allie Beth Allman & Associates.

Experience

Having moved nine times, including four corporate relocations to five different states - New York, Connecticut, Indiana, Florida and Texas - Susan understands how to assist buyers in all their needs. During her time in Florida, she began buying and selling real estate and managing several real estate properties. Through the years she has developed a thorough understanding of the buying and selling process. She realizes that every client has his or her own specific needs, concerns and questions. Her clients appreciate her strong sense of commitment and determination to find a wonderful home for them and their families.

Current Life

Susan currently resides in Westlake. She is a golf member at the prestigious Vaquero Club and currently serves on the House Committee. Susan is married to her husband Jimmy and has three children. Two currently attend Westlake Academy and her oldest son, a graduate from Westlake Academy currently attends University of Pennsylvania. Susan is an active parent and volunteer at Westlake Academy. She is also a member of Gateway Church and involved with various small groups there.
